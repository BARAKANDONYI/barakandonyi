About Me

My name is Baraka Ogongi Ndonyi, and I’m a dedicated web developer with a passion for building fast, clean, and interactive web experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into real-world applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tools.

I’m constantly learning and experimenting with new technologies to improve my skills and stay current in the evolving tech world. I’ve developed several projects, including Church Record Management System and Healthcare AI tool to enhance communication between patients and providers.

I believe that great websites are not just about code, they are about solving problems and creating meaningful experiences. When I’m not coding, I enjoy reading, solving logic puzzles, and exploring new tools in the developer space.
